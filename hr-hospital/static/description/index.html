Hospital Management System

Complete solution for managing hospital operations

A comprehensive hospital management module for Odoo 17 that helps manage doctors, patients, visits, diagnoses, and medical records efficiently.

Key Features

Doctor Management

  • Complete doctor profiles with specialities
  • Intern-mentor relationships
  • License tracking and experience calculation
  • Doctor schedule management
  • Rating system

Patient Management

  • Detailed patient records
  • Personal doctor assignment
  • Medical history tracking
  • Blood type and allergies
  • Insurance information

Visit Management

  • Schedule and track patient visits
  • Multiple visit types (primary, follow-up, emergency)
  • Visit status workflow
  • Visit rescheduling wizard
  • Calendar and Kanban views

Medical Diagnosis

  • Disease classification (ICD-10)
  • Diagnosis recording per visit
  • Severity levels
  • Approval workflow for interns
  • Treatment recommendations

Security & Access Control

Role-based access control with hierarchical groups:

  • Patient - View own visits only
  • Intern - View and edit own visits
  • Doctor - Manage own visits and intern visits
  • Manager - View all visits
  • Administrator - Full access with delete permissions

Reports & Analytics

  • Doctor report with visit history and patient list
  • Disease statistics with pivot and graph views
  • Visit analytics by doctor and status

Localization


Full Ukrainian translation included
